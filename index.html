JavaScript Mascot

A mascot for JavaScript.

A yellow creature with three eyes, holding a JavaScript flag.
A distant relative of the Lisp mascot.

The image in various sizes and formats can be found in Wikimedia Commons and in GitHub.

The mascot belongs to the community. Any contributions and any ideas or proposals for improvements or modifications are more than welcome! If you create any artwork using the mascot, please share it! javascriptmascot@gmail.com
